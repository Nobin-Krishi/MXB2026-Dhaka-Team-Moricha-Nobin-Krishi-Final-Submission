# Product Overview

## NobinKrishi (নবীন কৃষি) - Smart Agriculture Platform

NobinKrishi is a comprehensive smart farming platform designed for Bangladesh that leverages AI to help farmers increase profits, reduce crop losses, and connect directly with buyers. The platform eliminates middlemen and provides farmers with modern agricultural tools through mobile technology.

### Core Value Proposition
- **For Farmers**: Earn 45% more through direct sales, reduce crop losses by 30% via AI disease detection
- **For Consumers**: Pay 72% less than supermarket prices for fresh, local produce
- **For the Ecosystem**: Reduce food waste by 65% through efficient supply chain connections

### Key Features
1. **Smart Marketplace** - AI-powered matching between farmers and buyers
2. **Land Analysis** - AI recommendations for optimal crop selection based on soil and climate
3. **Demand Forecasting** - Predictive analytics for crop market demand
4. **Voice AI Assistant** - Bangla-language farming advice and support
5. **Disease Scanner** - Photo-based crop disease identification and treatment recommendations
6. **Weather Intelligence** - Hyperlocal weather forecasts for farming decisions
7. **Crop Knowledge Grid** - Comprehensive farming guides and market information

### Target Users
- **Primary**: Smallholder farmers in Bangladesh
- **Secondary**: Consumers seeking fresh local produce, restaurants, cooperatives
- **Tertiary**: Government agencies, NGOs, agricultural businesses

### Business Model
- Free for farmers to join and list crops
- 8% commission on successful marketplace transactions
- Premium analytics for large farms (₹500/month)
- Enterprise solutions for businesses and cooperatives
